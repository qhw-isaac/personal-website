Chilean Energy Transition

FRE 528 - Fall 2025

Econometric analysis of household heating choices in Chile, examining the transition from traditional firewood to modern energy sources. Used data from the CASEN surveys (2015-2022) combined with geospatial data from Hansen et al. (2013), applying methodology by Fercovic et al. (2024) to analyze the relationship between income, rural vs urban households, and heating fuel choice.
